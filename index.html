About me

  • Basic information

    Student of graphic design department at the Lviv Academy of Art. In 2017 participated in student exchange program.
    Studied at Eugeniusz Geppert academy of Fine Arts in Wroclaw.

    Partisipated in Erasmus students' exebition, printmaking exebition in Wroclaw. Took part in posters competitions and exebition "Święto Wrocławia" and "Jazz nad Odrą".

  • Scope of work

    Illustration, graphic design, typography, development of photography and media art.
